About

Not your typical backend guy.

Most engineers pick a lane. I picked three. I'm a backend engineer building cloud-native systems at HCL, a product maker shipping tools people actually depend on, and a graphic designer with a Behance full of branding work, Kufic calligraphy, and motion graphics.

I hold degrees in Commerce and Theology — which means I think about systems in ways most engineers don't. That mix of analytical rigor and philosophical depth is exactly what makes my backends different.

When I'm not architecting social graphs on Neo4j or building concurrent pipelines in Go, you'll find me designing logos, crafting Raycast extensions, or writing about tech on Medium.
